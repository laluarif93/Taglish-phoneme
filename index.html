Taglish Phoneme Collections

Purpose: Provide a reference of sounds of phonetic symbols to help annotating Taglish word-phoneme easier. Phonetic Symbols: We use 2-letter notation ARPABET-like. Stress is indicated by a digit immediately following a vowel (AA1, AH0, etc). No stress in a consonant. Stress Level:
0 : No Stress
1 : Primary Stress

if you feel there is a stress (even a little bit) in a syllable, put primary stress (1) instead of 0.
